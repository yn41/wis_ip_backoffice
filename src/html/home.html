About IPRankings.com

Hello world!

We’re a team of tech people in the patent industry who want to help a wider audience access and decipher patent data.

It’s our belief that patent valuation data can be extremely useful in making informed investment, business, and hiring decisions (just to name a few).

We hope that through our site, non-experts will be able to interact with patent data in a digestible way.

We’re starting off with US patent data, but are planning to incorporate global patent data in subsequent updates.
